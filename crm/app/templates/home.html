Key Features

✓ Lead Capture

Automatically capture leads from form submissions with auto-assigned agents and status tracking.

✓ Agent Assignment

Round-robin automatic assignment ensures fair distribution of leads across team members.

✓ 8-Stage Pipeline

Track leads through New Lead → Booked with visual status indicators and easy stage transitions.

✓ Visit Scheduling

Schedule property visits with date/time pickers and outcome tracking (Booked, Considering, Not Interested).

✓ Follow-up Reminders

Automatic detection of inactive leads with bulk reminder sending for timely follow-ups.

✓ Real-time Dashboard

View comprehensive analytics: lead counts, pipeline breakdown, visits, and bookings with auto-refresh.
